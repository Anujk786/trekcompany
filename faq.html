Frequently Asked Questions

What type of accommodation will be provided on treks?

+ -

On treks the accommodation can be in a hotel/homestay/lodge/guesthouse/tents etc. In local areas we try to provide accommodation in homestays and lodges to provide livelihood to the locals. It also helps you feel the life of mountains more closely.

How much prior should i book my trek?

+ -

It is advised to book your trek atleast 2 months prior so that we are able to make all the necessary arrangements to offer you a comfortable stay and the best stay experience with us. We take bookings even 1 week prior but that is subject to availabilty.

What are the rules of the cancellation policy ?

+ -

Our cancellation policy is quite simple you can cancel your stay with us upto 1 week prior to your booking dates and the full amount will be refunded back to your account but failing to do so will result in no refunding of the amount.

Which payment modes are accepted?

+ -

We accept all payment modes inclding UPI transfers and bank transfers such as NEFT and RTGS. Apart from these payment modes we accept all leading bank debit and credit cards. No cash is accepted or asked for upon arrival or during your stay.

How can i book my trek ?

+ -

Booking your trek and stay with us is quite simple, you just have to fill out your details in the contact us form given on our website and we'll revert back to you within 24 working hours in your E-mail with all the payment details or you can directly email to us at trekkingnomads2022@gmail.com and the same email will be used for any further discussion as well.
